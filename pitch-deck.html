HiveEmpire - the potentials

The problem: Is a bringing imperic data to very opaque process. And making "ownership" a tangible real-time feeling for bee-keepers who would normally have to wait weeks for feedback and results.

Our solution: By exposing the inner workings of the hive to bee-keepers we aim to enhance the sense of ownership. As well as bringing light and difficult to acquire knowledge to an ancient passtime and providing a sense of direction which is otherwise a blocker to all new beekeepers. This is accomplished by bringing a non-invasive, monitoring system that collects and provides data in real-time as well as eventually video.

Other Markets: There exists a potential to monetize and create a co-operative platform for the production and distribution of honey and thus tap into emerging organic locally produced oriented consumers.
