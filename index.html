Know your Hive

The best part of bee-keeping is getting to know your hive its bees & of course the Queen

HiveEmpire aims to get you even closer to this process & give you 24 hour low-impact monitoring. Giving your bees the space they need to do their thing, but also insight & access to precise hive metrics & data as it happens.
