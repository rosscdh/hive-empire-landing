About HiveEmpire

Who we are

We are a collection of technical topic specialists keen on getting involved in the beekeeping community.

Our aims include assisting academics gather data in this fascinating field and providing insign into the lives and processess of these fascinating creatures.

HiveEmpire aims to provide low-cost modular sensors and a revolutionary turn key monitoring solution.

Using machine-learning and expert knowledge we plan to provide you with imperic data-driven knowledge about your hive and the processes in play.

Using the power of community and co-operation we plan to make bee-keeping more than a hobby and more of an obsession.
